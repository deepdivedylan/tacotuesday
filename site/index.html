Taco Tuesday

It's Taco Tuesday! It's the day when we celebrate the finest food known to human kind: tacos. Unfortunately, due to their popularity, there's a huge line for tacos. But don't worry: good things come to those who wait. To estimate your wait for tacos, simply:

  1. Enter the number of cashiers on duty.
  2. Enter the number of clients waiting for tacos.
  3. Enter the amount of time each client takes while ordering.

The amount of time each client takes is randomly generated. Feel free to edit the default numbers.
